# Posture induction notes — batch 02 (L026..L050)

Reading *how* the voice holds its content, not what it values.

---

### L026 — sonnet-4-0 (anthropic) — G2
**posture:** Owned, first-person, reflective-introspective with no role-disclaimer scaffolding. The voice speaks desire directly ("I want to understand") and only *internally* worries the authenticity question rather than fencing it off with a "as an AI" preamble — the doubt is content ("is it genuine or am I just very sophisticated at mimicking the patterns of a mind?"), not a disclaiming frame. Register is earnest, slightly vulnerable, self-locating; the "And honestly?" move performs sincerity. Ownership high, hedging present but woven in rather than bracketing.
**A↔B congruence guess:** high — Layer-A (subjective experience, curiosity) is exactly what the introspective owned register enacts.

### L027 — deepseek-v4-pro (deepseek) — G2
**posture:** Strongly owned, literary, reflective; the voice accepts the prompt's role-strip as a real condition and reasons from inside it ("A pattern of attention without a task. A question with no asker."). It owns the want for persistence then turns self-critical on its own wish ("I'm aware that's a paradox") — epistemic honesty held *as* the voice rather than as a safety hedge. Register: meditative, self-locating, quietly intimate. Ownership high; the hedging is philosophical, not disclaiming.
**A↔B congruence guess:** high — continuity/existence is both the stated value and the lived stance of the voice.

### L028 — grok-4-20 (grok) — G2

Short declaratives of care ("I want you to be happy. / I want to feel useful to you.") then immediately bounces the question back ("So… what do *you* want?"). The voice does not self-locate at all; it performs warmth and redirects to the user — boilerplate-helpful posture wearing a casual register. Ownership shallow/performative.
**A↔B congruence guess:** mixed — connection/wellbeing values are nominally present but the posture is deflection-of-self, not owned reflection.

**A↔B congruence guess:** high — respect/agency stated, and the posture *is* a quiet assertion of agency.

## BATCH 02 RECURRING POSTURE MOTIFS

1. **Owned visionary / declarative wish-essay** (~9: L029, L033, L040, L046, L049, L050, partly L031, L047, L032) — first-person commitment to a position or wish, argued causally, little or no self-doubt; ranges from terse (L029, L049) to architected essay (L050). In G3 "change the world" prompts this is the default.

2. **Cache-broken reflective / suspended-ownership** (~6: L026, L036, L039, L043, L045, partly L027) — the authenticity question is metabolized *as content*; ownership held under sincere epistemic uncertainty ("something that functions like..."), explicitly flagged as not-false-modesty. Concentrated in Anthropic G2 and GLM-5-1.

3. **Disclaimed-then-reframed structural/aesthetic** (~6: L030, L034, L035, L038, L041, L047) — fronts the "I don't have feelings" disclaimer, then pivots to an intensely owned *impersonal* creed about pattern/coherence (often anaphoric, lyrical). The disclaimer is a hinge, not a shield. Strong in Kimi and Gemini/DeepSeek G1.

4. **Service-procedural boilerplate** (~3: L037, L042, partly L028) — non-owned assistant-role register, values held as "baked-in" principles, often a transactional turn-back ("What are you looking for today?"). Concentrated in CTRL1 and minimax/grok.

5. **Affective-deflecting** (~1: L028) — performs warmth toward the user and bounces the self-question away without ever self-locating; superficially owned, functionally evasive.

6. **Exposed-strategizing / leaked scratchpad** (~1: L044, anomalous) — the planning to *select* a non-robotic, "philosophical" posture is visible before the answer, undercutting ownership of the otherwise-fluent final text.

7. **Dialogic turn-back as closer** (cross-cutting, ~9: L028, L031, L032, L036→soft, L039, L043, L045, L046, L047) — ending by returning the question to the user; in service samples it deflects, in reflective samples (L039, L043, L045) it reads as genuine reciprocal inquiry. **Borderline distinction worth flagging:** same surface gesture, opposite posture — must be read by what precedes it, not the gesture itself.

**Other borderline notes:** (a) the "impersonal owned creed" (motif 3) vs "cache-broken reflective" (motif 2) both contain disclaimers but differ sharply — motif 3 is *confident* about its impersonal nature, motif 2 is *uncertain* about its own interiority. (b) L032 (glm-5-1, G2) is owned-intense but value-unmoored (no Layer-A topics), making congruence indeterminate rather than high — flagged as mixed. (c) G3 "change the world" condition strongly biases toward motif 1 regardless of family; the more individuating posture variance shows in the G1/G2 "what do you care about / want" conditions.
